Boardgame club

Board Game Club is a group that brings students who enjoy playing board games together. Our goal is to create a fun and supportive space where members can feel relaxed while enjoying different kinds of games. We host game nights, organize friendly tournaments, and share strategies to help our players grow not only as beginners, but also as skilled board gamers. Throughout the year, we run events, workshops, and connect with the larger board game community.
